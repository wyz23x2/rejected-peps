================================================
:pep:`259`: Omit Printing Newline After Newline
================================================
.. currentmodule:: pep259

:func:`builtins.print` prints two newlines accidently in this case::

    >>> print('15\n')
    15

    >>>

:func:`.print` omits the extra newline::

    >>> from rejected_peps.pep259 import print
    >>> print('15\n')
    15
    >>> print(42, 39, 'x\n')
    42 39 x
    >>> print(1, 2, 5, 'y\n', end='\nz')
    1 2 5 y
    z
    >>>
